132.Triangle Judgement

CODE:


select x,y,z ,
case when (x+y>z and y+z>x and x+z>y) then 'Yes'
else 'No' 
end as triangle
from Triangle


OUTPUT:

Example 1:

Input: 
Triangle table:
+----+----+----+
| x  | y  | z  |
+----+----+----+
| 13 | 15 | 30 |
| 10 | 20 | 15 |
+----+----+----+
Output: 
+----+----+----+----------+
| x  | y  | z  | triangle |
+----+----+----+----------+
| 13 | 15 | 30 | No       |
| 10 | 20 | 15 | Yes      |
+----+----+----+----------+
